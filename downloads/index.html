Other Resources

Events

Download

Events is the official PGM plugin for managing PvP matches in a competitive setting. Upon joining or cycling, all team members are forced onto their respective teams.

Community

Community is a standalone plugin for managing PGM servers. It includes various PGM features, such as punishments, moderation, freezing and other aspects.

ResourcePile

Visit

ResourcePile is a community project which aims to provide various collections of resources, such as maps or statistics, for users with backgrounds in Overcast and similar networks.

PGM Tracker

Visit

PGM Tracker is a community tool made by applenick that tracks public PGM servers. Take a look at it, play a variety of Minecraft PvP maps and get involved with development!
